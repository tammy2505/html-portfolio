The Best Movies According to Tamryn F

My Top 5 Movies of All Time

Anaconda: The Blood Orchid

This was one of the first movies I ever watched, and it's still my go-to when I'm bored.

It's a thrilling film with a solid cast, an engaging storyline, and even a few funny moments.

Dirty Dancing

This classic never gets old. Every time I watch it, I end up dancing in my room!

It's romantic, a bit dramatic, and always a great watch.

Grown Ups

One of the funniest movies and definitely one of Adam Sandler’s best. It’s always good for a laugh!

The Conjuring

A perfect mix of horror and suspense. One of the scariest movies out there—it'll have you hiding behind a pillow!

Titanic

Romantic and heart-wrenching, this epic film is long but unforgettable.
